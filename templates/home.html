Completely accurate rent paying simulation.

Click here to play.

Controls:

Arrow keys to move character, space to attack.

Objective:

Collect coins in the time remaining by killing skeletons in order to pay your rent.

Please Log In to be entered into leaderboards.

Please read terms of lease listed below.

  • Collect enough coins to pay the rent for your house.
  • Every time you complete a level you will be deducted a higher number of coins.
  • Days and the amount of spawning skeletons per map section are proportional.
  • One skeleton is equal to one coin.
  • Clicking to begin will sign your lease contract and throw you into a field containing infinitely spawning skeletons.
  • You will have 1 and a half minutes to collect enough coins to pay your rent each day.
  • Losing your hearts or failure to pay the rent will result in a Game Over.
  • Arrow keys to move and Space Bar is to attack.
